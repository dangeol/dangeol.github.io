  1. Download a text or html-file containing pāḷi words (e.g. from here) and click on the left button to load it.
  2. Click on a pāḷi word in the text or in the hit list to show its translation.
  3. Optional: Use the range slider to change the number of the most frequently found pāḷi words to be shown (default: 15). Click then on the "Refresh" button.
